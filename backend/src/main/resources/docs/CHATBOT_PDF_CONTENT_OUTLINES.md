# Chatbot PDF Content Outlines For E-Bike

Tai lieu nay la bo suon noi dung de nhom viet cac PDF lam nguon tri thuc cho chatbot e-bike.

## Cach dung

- Moi muc `PDF` ben duoi tuong ung voi 1 file PDF de nhom bien soan.
- Giu van phong don gian, ro nghia, it marketing, uu tien thong tin ma chatbot co the trich ra de tra loi.
- Moi doan ngan nen chi chua 1 y chinh.
- Neu mot thong tin thay doi thuong xuyen nhu gia ban, ton kho, khuyen mai ngan han, showroom dang hoat dong: khong nen coi PDF la nguon chinh.
- Moi PDF nen co:
  - Ten tai lieu
  - Pham vi ap dung
  - Ngay cap nhat
  - Phien ban tai lieu
  - Don vi phu trach xac nhan noi dung

## Nguyen tac viet de chatbot de dung

- Dung cau khang dinh ro rang, tranh viet mo ho.
- Neu co dieu kien ap dung, viet thanh bullet cu the.
- Neu co quy trinh, viet theo thu tu buoc 1, 2, 3.
- Neu co ngoai le, viet rieng thanh muc `Luu y` hoac `Truong hop khong ap dung`.
- Dung tu ngu ma khach hang hay hoi that su: `bao hanh`, `sac bao lau`, `di duoc bao nhieu km`, `co giao tan noi khong`, `co tra gop khong`.
- Tranh dua qua nhieu bang bieu phuc tap vao PDF neu no co the viet thanh doan ngan de chatbot de trich xuat hon.

## PDF 01: Gioi Thieu Thuong Hieu Va Dich Vu

Ten file de xuat: `01-gioi-thieu-thuong-hieu-va-dich-vu.pdf`

### Muc tieu

- Giup chatbot tra loi cac cau hoi tong quan ve doanh nghiep.
- Giai thich doanh nghiep ban gi, phuc vu ai, diem khac biet la gi.
- Tao lop context nen cho cac cau hoi mo dau cua nguoi dung.

### Chatbot can tra loi duoc

- Kinetic la ai?
- Kinetic ban nhung loai xe nao?
- Doi tuong khach hang chinh la ai?
- Diem khac biet cua Kinetic la gi?
- Kinetic co nhung dich vu ho tro nao sau mua?

### Suon noi dung de viet

#### 1. Gioi thieu ngan gon

- Ten thuong hieu:
- Linh vuc hoat dong:
- San pham chinh:
- Thi truong phuc vu:
- Tam nhin ngan gon:

Mau viet:

`Kinetic la thuong hieu chuyen cung cap ...`

#### 2. Khach hang muc tieu

- Nhom khach hang di hoc
- Nhom khach hang di lam hang ngay
- Nhom khach hang can tiet kiem chi phi di chuyen
- Nhom khach hang uu tien thiet ke, van hanh em, tiet kiem dien

Mau viet:

`Doi voi khach di hoc, Kinetic phu hop khi ...`

#### 3. Nhom san pham dang cung cap

- Xe dap dien
- Xe may dien
- Phu kien ho tro neu co

Can mo ta cho moi nhom:

- Dac diem tong quan
- Truong hop su dung phu hop
- Diem manh
- Diem can luu y

#### 4. Gia tri va diem khac biet

- Uu diem ve thiet ke
- Uu diem ve van hanh
- Uu diem ve chi phi su dung
- Uu diem ve hau mai
- Uu diem ve kenh ho tro

#### 5. Dich vu di kem

- Tu van chon xe
- Ho tro dat hang
- Ho tro thanh toan
- Ho tro nhan xe
- Huong dan su dung ban dau
- Bao hanh va cham soc sau mua

#### 6. Kenh lien he va ho tro

- Hotline
- Email
- Fanpage
- Website
- Showroom
- Khung gio ho tro

#### 7. Luu y ve pham vi thong tin

- Cac noi dung gioi thieu chi mang tinh tong quan.
- Gia ban, ton kho, khuyen mai va showroom dang hoat dong xem tren he thong cap nhat.

## PDF 02: Huong Dan Chon Xe Dien

Ten file de xuat: `02-huong-dan-chon-xe-dien.pdf`

### Muc tieu

- Giup chatbot tu van chon xe theo nhu cau.
- Bien nhung tu van thuong gap thanh noi dung co cau truc.
- Giam viec chatbot tra loi chung chung.

### Chatbot can tra loi duoc

- Nen chon xe dap dien hay xe may dien?
- Ngan sach nay nen chon dong nao?
- Di hoc, di lam, di gan, di xa thi chon xe gi?
- Can uu tien toc do, pin, range hay tai trong?
- Nguoi moi di xe dien can luu y gi?

### Suon noi dung de viet

#### 1. Cach xac dinh nhu cau

- Di bao nhieu km moi ngay
- Duong di bang phang hay nhieu doc cau
- Co can cho hang hay cho them nguoi khong
- Uu tien toc do hay tiet kiem
- Co cho sac thuong xuyen tai nha hay noi lam viec khong
- Ngan sach du kien

#### 2. Khi nao nen chon xe dap dien

- Phu hop voi doi tuong nao
- Uu diem
- Han che
- Tinh huong su dung mau

Mau viet:

`Xe dap dien phu hop voi nguoi dung co quang duong di chuyen ...`

#### 3. Khi nao nen chon xe may dien

- Phu hop voi doi tuong nao
- Uu diem
- Han che
- Tinh huong su dung mau

#### 4. Tu van theo ngan sach

- Nhom ngan sach tiet kiem
- Nhom ngan sach tam trung
- Nhom ngan sach uu tien trai nghiem cao hon

Cho moi nhom, viet:

- Mong doi hop ly
- Loai xe phu hop
- Tieu chi nen uu tien
- Tieu chi co the danh doi

#### 5. Tu van theo muc dich su dung

- Di hoc
- Di lam hang ngay
- Chay trong noi thanh
- Di quang duong xa hon muc trung binh
- Uu tien thiet ke nho gon
- Uu tien cho nguoi moi su dung

#### 6. Cac tieu chi can so sanh

- Quang duong toi da moi lan sac
- Toc do toi da
- Loai pin
- Thoi gian sac
- Tai trong
- Chieu cao yen
- Chi phi van hanh
- Muc do de bao duong

Mau viet:

`Neu nguoi dung di gan moi ngay, quang duong toi da khong phai tieu chi quan trong nhat.`

#### 7. Nhung hieu lam thuong gap

- Range cong bo va range thuc te khac nhau the nao
- Sac nhanh khong phai luc nao cung tot hon
- Toc do cao khong dong nghia voi phu hop moi nguoi dung
- Gia cao hon khong co nghia la phu hop hon trong moi truong hop

#### 8. Goi y cau hoi de chatbot hoi nguoc nguoi dung

- Ngan sach cua ban khoang bao nhieu?
- Ban di khoang bao nhieu km moi ngay?
- Ban uu tien xe dap dien hay xe may dien?
- Ban can xe nho gon hay uu tien hieu nang?

## PDF 03: Chinh Sach Ban Hang Va Thanh Toan

Ten file de xuat: `03-chinh-sach-ban-hang-va-thanh-toan.pdf`

### Muc tieu

- Giup chatbot tra loi ro ve quy trinh mua hang.
- Giam nham lan trong cac cau hoi lien quan den dat hang, thanh toan, nhan xe.

### Chatbot can tra loi duoc

- Muon mua xe thi lam nhu the nao?
- Can cung cap nhung thong tin gi khi dat hang?
- Co nhung hinh thuc thanh toan nao?
- Nhan xe tai dau?
- Sau khi thanh toan thi don hang duoc xu ly ra sao?

### Suon noi dung de viet

#### 1. Pham vi chinh sach

- Chinh sach ap dung cho kenh nao: website, showroom, kenh online khac
- Doi tuong ap dung
- Khu vuc ap dung

#### 2. Quy trinh dat hang

1. Chon san pham
2. Cung cap thong tin nguoi mua
3. Chon hinh thuc nhan xe
4. Xac nhan thong tin
5. Thanh toan hoac dat coc neu co
6. He thong tiep nhan va xu ly
7. Showroom hoac bo phan lien quan lien he xac nhan

#### 3. Thong tin khach hang can cung cap

- Ho va ten
- So dien thoai
- Email
- Giay to can thiet neu quy trinh yeu cau
- Dia chi lien he
- Ghi chu them

Neu co rang buoc:

- Truong thong tin nao bat buoc
- Truong hop thieu thong tin thi don hang xu ly the nao

#### 4. Hinh thuc thanh toan

- Thanh toan online
- Chuyen khoan
- Cong thanh toan nhu VNPay neu co
- Thanh toan theo quy dinh tai showroom neu co

Can viet ro:

- Don hang duoc xem la thanh cong khi nao
- Truong hop giao dich loi thi xu ly the nao
- Khach hang can lam gi neu bi tru tien nhung chua nhan xac nhan

#### 5. Quy trinh nhan xe

- Nhan tai showroom hay kenh khac
- Cach xac nhan lich nhan xe
- Giay to can mang theo
- Buoc kiem tra xe khi nhan
- Cac noi dung can ky nhan neu co

#### 6. Hoa don va xac nhan mua hang

- Co gui email xac nhan hay khong
- Co hoa don khong
- Thoi diem gui xac nhan
- Cach khach hang kiem tra trang thai don

#### 7. Truong hop can luu y

- Don hang thanh toan that bai
- Don hang cho xac nhan
- Thay doi lich nhan xe
- Sai thong tin lien he
- Muon huy yeu cau sau khi da tao don

#### 8. Nhung noi dung khong nen de co dinh trong PDF

- Gia cu the cua tung mau xe
- Ma khuyen mai ngan han
- Tinh trang ton kho theo thoi diem

## PDF 04: Bao Hanh Doi Tra Va Hau Mai

Ten file de xuat: `04-bao-hanh-doi-tra-va-hau-mai.pdf`

### Muc tieu

- Lam nguon tra loi tin cay cho cac cau hoi sau mua hang.
- Giam hallucination khi chatbot tra loi ve bao hanh, doi tra.

### Chatbot can tra loi duoc

- Xe duoc bao hanh nhu the nao?
- Pin, dong co, khung xe co bao hanh khong?
- Truong hop nao khong duoc bao hanh?
- Muon yeu cau bao hanh thi lam sao?
- Co doi tra hoac hoan tien khong?
- Sau mua thi duoc ho tro gi?

### Suon noi dung de viet

#### 1. Nguyen tac chung

- Chinh sach ap dung cho san pham nao
- Thoi diem bat dau tinh bao hanh
- Can cu theo hoa don, phieu mua hang, serial, ma don hang hay thong tin nao

#### 2. Pham vi bao hanh

Can tach ro tung nhom:

- Pin
- Dong co
- Bo dieu khien
- Khung xe
- Linh kien dien
- Phu kien di kem

Cho moi nhom, viet:

- Co bao hanh hay khong
- Thoi han bao hanh
- Dieu kien ap dung
- Ghi chu dac biet

#### 3. Cac truong hop khong duoc bao hanh

- Hao mon tu nhien
- Loi do su dung sai cach
- Tu y sua chua, thay doi ket cau
- Hong do nuoc, va dap, tai nan
- Hong do su dung sac khong phu hop
- Khong co thong tin mua hang hop le

#### 4. Quy trinh tiep nhan bao hanh

1. Khach hang lien he kenh ho tro
2. Cung cap thong tin don hang va tinh trang loi
3. Bo phan tiep nhan huong dan
4. Dua xe den diem kiem tra hoac lam theo huong dan
5. Xac dinh tinh trang bao hanh
6. Thong bao phuong an xu ly
7. Ban giao lai xe sau xu ly

#### 5. Thoi gian xu ly du kien

- Truong hop kiem tra nhanh
- Truong hop can dat linh kien
- Truong hop can chuyen ve trung tam ky thuat

Neu chua chot duoc so ngay cu the, viet theo kieu:

`Thoi gian xu ly phu thuoc vao muc do loi va tinh san sang cua linh kien.`

#### 6. Chinh sach doi tra

- Dieu kien xem xet doi tra
- Moc thoi gian xem xet
- Yeu cau ve tinh trang xe
- Giay to can co
- Chi phi phat sinh neu co
- Truong hop khong ap dung doi tra

#### 7. Chinh sach hoan tien neu co

- Dieu kien hoan tien
- Cach tinh muc hoan tien
- Hinh thuc hoan tien
- Thoi gian du kien

#### 8. Hau mai sau mua

- Bao duong dinh ky
- Kiem tra xe
- Ho tro ky thuat
- Tu van su dung dung cach
- Cac kenh lien he khi gap su co

#### 9. Luu y phap ly va van hanh

- Noi dung cuoi cung duoc xac nhan theo chinh sach hien hanh cua doanh nghiep.
- Thong tin chi tiet co the duoc doi chieu them tai showroom hoac bo phan bao hanh.

## PDF 05: Huong Dan Su Dung Sac Pin Va An Toan

Ten file de xuat: `05-huong-dan-su-dung-sac-pin-va-an-toan.pdf`

### Muc tieu

- Giu chatbot tra loi chinh xac ve cach su dung co ban.
- Han che tra loi nguy hiem trong cac cau hoi lien quan pin, sac, ngap nuoc, an toan.

### Chatbot can tra loi duoc

- Sac pin nhu the nao cho dung?
- Sac bao lau thi hop ly?
- Co nen sac qua dem khong?
- Di mua, di ngap nuoc can luu y gi?
- Bao quan xe khi khong su dung lau ngay the nao?
- Khi co dau hieu bat thuong thi can lam gi?

### Suon noi dung de viet

#### 1. Nguyen tac su dung an toan

- Doc huong dan truoc khi van hanh
- Su dung dung muc dich
- Khong tu y sua doi bo phan dien
- Su dung bo sac phu hop

#### 2. Huong dan sac pin

- Sac o noi kho rao, thong thoang
- Kiem tra nguon dien truoc khi sac
- Trinh tu cam sac neu can quy dinh
- Khoang thoi gian sac thong thuong
- Dau hieu sac xong
- Nhung dieu khong nen lam khi sac

Can viet ro cac canh bao:

- Khong dung bo sac khong ro nguon goc
- Khong sac o noi am uot
- Khong de vat de chay gan khu vuc sac

#### 3. Bao quan pin

- Nhiet do moi truong phu hop
- Neu khong dung xe trong thoi gian dai thi can lam gi
- Tan suat kiem tra muc pin
- Khi nao can mang di kiem tra

#### 4. Van hanh hang ngay

- Kiem tra phanh, den, lop, muc pin
- Khoi dong va tat xe dung quy trinh neu co
- Cac luu y khi cho tai
- Cac luu y khi di duong tron, mua, dem

#### 5. Di mua va ngap nuoc

- Muc do tiep xuc nuoc can tranh
- Khong nen lam gi khi xe vua di qua vung ngap
- Khi nao can ngung su dung va lien he ho tro

#### 6. Dau hieu bat thuong can xu ly ngay

- Pin nong bat thuong
- Mui khe, khoi, tieng la
- Xe sut range bat thuong
- Sac khong vao
- Den bao loi bat thuong neu co

Mau viet:

`Neu phat hien mui khe, khoi hoac nhiet do bat thuong, ngung su dung ngay va lien he bo phan ho tro.`

#### 7. Bao duong co ban

- Ap suat lop
- He thong phanh
- Den va coi
- Ve sinh xe
- Kiem tra dau noi, day dien theo khuyen nghi

#### 8. Cac cau hoi thuong gap

- Sac bao lau?
- Sac qua dem duoc khong?
- Moi lan sac di duoc bao nhieu km?
- Co nen dung den khi pin ve 0 khong?
- Lau khong di co anh huong pin khong?

#### 9. Muc tu choi cua chatbot

Noi dung nen co de chatbot biet gioi han:

- Khong huong dan thao lap pin neu khach hang khong phai ky thuat vien.
- Khong khuyen khich tu sua mach dien.
- Khuyen khich lien he bo phan ky thuat khi co rui ro an toan.
